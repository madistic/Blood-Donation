Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our highest priorities in the Blood Bank Management System

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our Blood Bank Management System. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection.

Privacy Commitment

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or medical emergency protocols.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide safe and effective blood bank services:
  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, age, and identification
  • Medical Information: Blood type, medical history, and health screening results
  • Donation Records: Donation history, dates, and locations
  • Account Information: Login credentials and account preferences
  • Usage Data: System interactions and access logs for security

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for legitimate blood bank operations:
  • Processing and managing blood donations and requests
  • Ensuring medical safety and compatibility
  • Maintaining donation records and medical histories
  • Communicating important health and safety information
  • Improving our services and system security
  • Complying with medical and legal requirements

Data Security Measures

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your sensitive information:
SSL Encryption Secure Servers Access Controls Audit Trails
  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls for staff and administrators
  • Automatic logout and session management
  • Secure data backup and disaster recovery protocols

Information Sharing

We only share your information in specific, limited circumstances:
  • Medical Emergencies: Critical information shared with healthcare providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court orders
  • Safety Concerns: To prevent harm to individuals or public health
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize information sharing

No Commercial Use

We never use your personal information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purposes unrelated to blood bank operations.

Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive rights regarding your personal information:
  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to medical/legal requirements)
  • Portability: Export your data in a common format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to improve your experience and ensure system functionality:
  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and basic functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • No Tracking: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for medical, legal, and operational purposes:
  • Active account data: While your account remains active
  • Medical records: As required by healthcare regulations
  • Donation history: Permanently for safety and traceability
  • Inactive accounts: Archived after 2 years of inactivity
